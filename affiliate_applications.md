⚠️ **International (Italy) payee specifics:**
- Most US affiliate networks (Impact, CJ, Awin) require a **W-8BEN** form before they release payment. This is the IRS form that declares you're a non-US person and claim treaty benefits to reduce/eliminate US withholding tax. Italy has a US tax treaty so this is straightforward.

- Payment minimums vary ($25–$100 typical). Wise transfers convert to EUR with the lowest fees.

### F. Monetization model

> Affiliate revenue is FinBrief's exclusive monetization model. We do not run display ads, paid placements, or sponsored content. All affiliate links are routed through a server-side redirect at `/go/[partner]` (disallowed in robots.txt) that logs clicks for attribution. The redirect resolves the destination URL from a server-only environment variable and appends a subid for partner postback. Real affiliate URLs are never embedded in client-rendered HTML.
>
> Every page that contains a monetized link displays the FTC-required affiliate disclosure above the fold. Disclosure boilerplate, our methodology, and our editorial-independence policy are published at https://finbrief.space/about#disclosure and https://finbrief.space/editorial-standards.

We publish our ranking methodology so readers can verify how products are scored, and we explicitly state in our editorial standards that we rank lower-paying partners above higher-paying ones when the product is the better fit. We do not accept advertiser direction on editorial content.

### H. Editorial methodology summary

> Every FinBrief article goes through a three-step review process before publishing: (1) Source check — every number, date, and statutory rule is matched against a primary source linked from our internal source register (IRS news releases, SEC/FINRA filings, FDIC data, official institutional disclosures); (2) Clarity check — does the article actually answer the reader's question without jargon walls; (3) Affiliate-independence check — does the product ranking reflect actual fit, not affiliate payout size. Full standards documented at https://finbrief.space/editorial-standards.
